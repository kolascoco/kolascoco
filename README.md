## Research

I study cortical excitability and connectivity in humans, combining transcranial magnetic stimulation with EEG. My work centres on the methodology that makes these recordings interpretable — artifact handling, sensory control conditions, and reproducible preprocessing — alongside brain–computer interfaces and sensorimotor neurophysiology.

**Interests:** TMS-EEG · TMS-evoked potentials · neurophysiology · brain–computer interfaces · reproducible analysis pipelines
